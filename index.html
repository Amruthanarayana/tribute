Tribute to Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

About Dr. Kalam

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. Known as the "Missile Man of India," he played a pivotal role in India's civilian space and military missile programs.

He was a visionary leader and an inspiration for millions around the world. His dedication to education and innovation continues to guide the youth of India.
